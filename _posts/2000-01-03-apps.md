## Apps

### Polo Exchange

[![Polo Exchange](img/icon-polo.png)](https://itunes.apple.com/app/apple-store/id1234141021?mt=8)

Easy access to the Poloniex cryptocurrency exchange on your mobile.

* Real time ticker updates on all markets
* Manage favorites and search for currencies
* Manage alerts and get notified about price changes
* Candle stick charts in different time intervals
* Access account information to see your balances and orders
* Two today extensions to show your favorites and your account balances
* Dark mode

### CryptoRadar

[![CryptoRadar](img/icon-cryptoradar.png)](https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/cryptoradar/id1315274114?mt=8)

You want to know when a new coin gets listed on an exchange? This App will send you a push notification when this happens. All you need to do is select the exchanges you're interested in, that's it! There are 84 exchanges supported!
