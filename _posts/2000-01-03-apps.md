## Apps

### CryptoRadar

[![CryptoRadar](img/icon-cryptoradar.png)](https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/cryptoradar/id1315274114?mt=8)

You want to know when a new coin gets listed on an exchange? This App will send you a push notification when this happens. All you need to do is select the exchanges you're interested in, that's it! There are 84 exchanges supported!
